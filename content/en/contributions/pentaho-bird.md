Penatho Birt plugin is an open-source plugin designed to integrate BIRT (Business Intelligence and Reporting Tools) with the Pentaho platform. It enables users to create, manage, and generate reports seamlessly within the Pentaho environment, leveraging BIRT's powerful reporting capabilities.
