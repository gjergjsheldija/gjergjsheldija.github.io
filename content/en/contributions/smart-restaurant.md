Smart Restaurant is an open-source Restaurant Management System designed to streamline restaurant operations. It includes features such as order management, table reservations, billing, and inventory tracking. The system is built with a focus on usability and efficiency, making it easier for restaurant staff to manage daily tasks and enhance customer service.
